Favorite Games

Shogun 2

This game is just fantastic. It puts you in medival japan and you can fight as one of 12 rulers listed in the game. Your goal in this game is to defeat other local rulers and become the military ruler of japan

Hearts of iron IV

This game takes place 3 years before the WWII. You have to choose a nation like germany or USA or Soviets. this game has a good UI design and any history buff will like this game. whether it is to conquer the world as germany or liberate europe as Americans, this game will not dissapoint.
